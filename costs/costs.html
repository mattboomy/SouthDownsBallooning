Cost of a hot air balloon ride for singles and partners over the South Downs

Please be aware that ballooning is an outdoor activity usually in the open countryside. Participants are required to have a degree of fitness and mobility. In the interest of safety in an emergency no passenger will be flown if they are unable to demonstrate that they can board and depart the basket unaided. To avoid overloading the balloon passengers need to be 15stone (100kg) or less. We can check weights on the launch site.

To purchase a hot air balloon flight online by debit or credit card or with your PayPal account choose the appropriate ticket description below and add PASSENGERS(S) name(s) and then click on the "Buy Now" button. This will take you to secure website.

We fly over the Hampshire Sussex border and choose the appropriate launchsite within the area on the day. See individual web pages for details. We cannot guarantee that you will fly from a specific launch site so you must be prepared to travel. Morning flights go from either West Meon or Farnham only.

e-ticket

Flight Vouchers are normally "Open" dated. Alternatively call or email us for specific dates.

If you use your PayPal account rather than a Debit or Credit card they keep your details safe but they will give us your email address.

Postal ticket

Choose the appropriate option and add the name(s) of the lucky PASSENGERS(S) that we should use on the flight voucher. Then click the "Buy Now" button and complete the fields provided on PayPal for your address. Then email us or call to specify that you would like the Flight Voucher delivered by post.
