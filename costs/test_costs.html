Cost of a hot air balloon ride for singles and partners over the South Downs

To purchase a hot air balloon flight online by debit or credit card click on the "Continue to payment" button on the ticket description that you require below. This will take you to secure website.

NOTE:

1. Your details as the purchaser go in the "Billing" section. Fill in your address for postal ticket or email address if you require an e-ticket.

2. If you are purchasing the ticket for a friend or relation click the "Postal Address", "Enter a different address" radio button and enter their name, town and Post Code in these columns.

The ticket will be issued with the appropriate name and an open date and launch site. You or they then call or email us to book on the flight at an appropriate date and venue.

 

Weekday single am £105

Weekday single pm £125

Weekend single flight £145

 

Weekday couple am £210

Weekday couple pm £250

Weekend couple flight £290

 

Payment by Cheque

If you prefer to pay by cheque please make it out to "South Downs Ballooning Ltd" and send by post to South Downs ballooning, 3 Chalcraft Close, Liphook, Hampshire GU30 7PW and include your name, address and either email address or telephone number.

To pay by telephone

Call us on 01428 725696 if you prefer to book and pay on the phone.

Privacy Policy

We do not store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any third parties.

Delivery Policy

Vouchers for your balloon flight will be despatched by either Royal Mail or email normaly within two but in no more than five working days of receipt of your order.
